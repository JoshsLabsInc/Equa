Equa – adaptive AI math learning engine

Every student deserves a personal math coach

Josh Sooknanan · project blueprint working title

Equa is an AI-powered mathematics learning engine designed to reduce high failure rates in university-level math. It replaces passive lectures with mastery-based progression, personalized coaching and structured engagement.

Sample AI coach message: “Hi Alex, I see you're practicing derivatives. Last time you struggled with the power rule. Want to try a warm‑up?”
Your coach, Maya (customizable avatar) · memory persistent

Many students struggle not because they lack intelligence, but because they lack structured practice and confidence. Equa bridges that gap.

⚠️ Problem & diagnosis

Weak foundations

Students arrive with gaps from secondary education. Passive lectures don't fix that.
75% struggle with limits

High‑stakes exams

Heavy reliance on midterms/finals, minimal formative feedback. Math anxiety rises.

No personalised guidance

Instructors cannot give individual feedback to 300+ students. Many fall behind early.

Embarrassment to ask

Peer pressure & shame block tutoring usage. Equa’s private AI coach removes that barrier.

⚡ VS mode · practice with light competition

AI opponent

Solve the same problem as a simulated bot. It uses average solve time adjusted by your ELO.

Opponent chat: “Thinking…” · “Simplifying…” · “Almost there…”

No grades, just fun repetition. Your AI coach can still give hints.

ELO‑balanced

Lower skill = more time assistance, so you stay encouraged. Great for daily streaks.

🏆 Ranked mode · ELO progression

ELO from zero

Win ≈ +25 points, bonus for speed. Step‑by‑step validation prevents guessing.

Timed & randomized

No two ranked matches are the same — every equation uses dynamic parameters (a·xⁿ).

Ranked may eventually contribute to course grades (phase 3). Anti‑cheating: tab‑switch detection & limited hints.
